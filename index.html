Brower Electric

Brower Electric, a 3rd generation business founded in 1978, provides top-quality electrical services to Missouri and some surrounding areas. Owned and operated by the Brower family, the team at Brower Electric is dedicated to delivering exceptional customer service and ensuring customer satisfaction.

Our Services

COMMERCIAL

Brower Electric offers commercial electric services, specializing in new construction. Our team of experienced electricians can handle installations, repairs, maintenance, and inspections for your business. We strive to provide exceptional service and ensure complete customer satisfaction.
